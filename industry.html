Industry of Interest

I am interested in the automotive industry because it combines technology, engineering, and performance. This industry includes vehicle design, manufacturing, racing, and innovation in areas such as safety, speed, and efficiency. I especially enjoy how it connects everyday transportation with high-performance motorsports like Formula One.

Career of Interest

One career path that interests me is working as a race engineer for a Formula One team. A race engineer analyzes car performance, communicates with drivers, and makes decisions that improve speed and handling. This career is exciting because it involves teamwork, fast decision-making, and advanced technology.

Ethical Issue

One ethical issue in the automotive and racing industry is fairness. Some teams may have more resources or advanced technology, which can create an unfair advantage. It is important to follow rules honestly and maintain integrity in competition.

How My Values Shape My Response

My personal values of faith, community, respect, and forgiveness influence how I respond to ethical issues. Respect helps me value fairness, community reminds me of teamwork, faith guides my decisions, and forgiveness allows me to learn from mistakes and improve.

Cybersecurity Issues

Cybersecurity issues in the automotive industry include hacking, data theft, and unauthorized access to vehicle systems. In Formula One, protecting engineering data and team strategies is critical because it can impact performance and safety.

Cybersecurity Best Practices

Best practices include using strong passwords, securing networks, limiting access to sensitive data, regularly updating software, and training employees to recognize cyber threats. These steps help protect important information and maintain safety in the industry.
