My Core Values

Values | Industry

Faith

1. Faith is a core part of who I am and guides my decisions in life.

2. This value comes from my upbringing and personal beliefs.

3. Faith gives me purpose, direction, and strength during difficult times.

4. Without faith, I may feel lost and struggle to stay grounded in my values.

Community

1. I value being part of a supportive and connected community.

2. This comes from my experiences with family, friends, and those around me.

3. Community creates a sense of belonging, support, and shared growth.

4. Without community, I could feel isolated and miss meaningful relationships.

Respect

1. I believe in treating everyone with dignity and fairness.

2. This value comes from my family and how I was raised.

3. Respect builds trust and strong relationships with others.

4. Without respect, relationships can break down and lead to conflict.

Forgiveness

1. I believe in forgiving others and myself to move forward.

2. This value comes from personal experiences and growth.

3. Forgiveness promotes healing, peace, and emotional well-being.

4. Without forgiveness, I may hold onto anger that harms my relationships.
